Welcome to Iraq Youth Scientists Team

IYST is the first youth-led science initiative in Iraq, dedicated to empowering the next generation of scientists and innovators. IYST provides young Iraqis with opportunities to explore scientific fields, engage in research, and collaborate on innovative projects. Through workshops, competitions, and community-driven events, the team aims to inspire curiosity, foster creativity, and contribute to Iraq’s scientific advancement.

Our Vision

To cultivate a generation of young Iraqi scientists and innovators who lead breakthroughs in science and technology, contributing to the sustainable development and global recognition of Iraq as a hub for scientific excellence.

Our Mission

To empower Iraqi youth by providing access to quality scientific education, hands-on research opportunities, and a collaborative community. IYST aims to inspire innovation, nurture critical thinking, and bridge the gap between science and society, fostering a culture of discovery and progress in Iraq.

Our Impact

Since our inception, IYST has:

  • Supported over 200 young people.
  • Organized 20+ online and in-person events and workshops.
  • Published 7+ research papers.
  • Contributed to the personal growth and professional development of our members.
